# About us

This toolkit was developed by the [Association for Learning Technology
Digital Sustainability SIG](https://www.alt.ac.uk/digital-sustainability-sig). We are individuals from several different UK Universities who wanted to do something about the role of Educational Technology in the Climate and Ecological crisis.\
\
The toolkit was developed after running a series of workshops on Digital
Education ​and the climate and ecological emergency​. The workshops aimed
to;

-   Encourage reflection on how to influence structural change in our
    potential spheres of influence.
-   Crowdsource what we already have and what is missing that we would
    like.
-   Start planning what could help us move forward and how we could
    collaborate to increase our influence and impact.

We hope that this toolkit will help continue the conversation at your
own institution and between institutions by providing a structure to
work collaboratively and bring about meaningful change.
