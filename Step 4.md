# Step 4: Hold the conversation

with, have a conversation within your wider institution. If you decide
to run a workshop you can use the workshop plan to discuss, reflect and
prioritize collective action within your organisation or field (see
workshop slides and template for collaborative document for you to adapt
as appropriate).
