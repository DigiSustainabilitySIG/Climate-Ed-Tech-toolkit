Step 3: Collaborate with colleagues

Find out how to engage your colleagues in the discussion and work out who to get involved.
We work better together, whether in a team, department, organization, or across organizations.

Consider your context and determine the initial level of your collaboration:

  • How might you find like-minded colleague/s?
  • How can you make an impact in your immediate team?
  • What activity can you connect with or lead on in your wider team / department?
  • A bigger part of the organisation
  • How can you support sustainability activity in the whole organisation?
